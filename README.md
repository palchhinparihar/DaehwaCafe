***About DAEHWA Cafe Community*

*Who We Are*

*DAEHWA Cafe Community is an independent community that connects people through Korean culture, education, creativity, and meaningful collaboration. We welcome everyone who has an interest in Korea and dreams of exploring new opportunities through cultural exchange, learning, and community activities.*

*Our goal is to build a supportive environment where members can learn, grow, develop their skills, and help others grow together.*

*Our Mission*

* *To connect people who are interested in Korea through education, culture, and community.*

* *To create opportunities for learning, networking, and personal growth.*

* *To encourage youth leadership, creativity, and volunteerism.*

* *To organize meaningful activities that strengthen cultural understanding between India and Korea.*

***About DAEHWA Cafe.TalkRoom*

*Who We Are*

*DAEHWA Cafe.TalkRoom is a Korean language learning platform created by our Founder & Director, Sandeep Singh, with the vision of making Korean language education more practical, affordable, and accessible for everyone.*

*Our primary goal is not only to teach Korean grammar and writing but also to help learners build the confidence to communicate naturally in Korean while understanding Korean culture and preparing for future opportunities.*

*Our Mission*

* *To provide high-quality and affordable Korean language education.*

* *To help learners improve their Korean speaking skills with confidence.*

* *To guide students who dream of studying, working, or building a future related to Korea.*

* *To make Korean language learning accessible to students from every part of India.*

*Our Vision*

*To become a trusted Korean language learning platform that empowers students with language skills, cultural understanding, and practical guidance for opportunities related to Korea.*

*What We Offer*

*Korean Language Classes*

*Structured Korean language courses for beginners to advanced learners, focusing on practical communication and real-life usage.*

*Speaking-Focused Learning*

Special emphasis on improving pronunciation, conversation skills, confidence, and natural communication in Korean.

*TOPIK Preparation*

Guidance and preparation support for students planning to appear for the TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) examination.

*Study in Korea Guidance*

Information and guidance for students interested in studying in Korea, including the admission process, scholarships, universities, and student life.

*Korean Culture Education*

Interactive learning about Korean traditions, etiquette, festivals, history, lifestyle, and contemporary culture.

*Career Guidance Related to Korea*

Support and guidance for learners exploring careers connected to Korea, including language-related professions and other opportunities.

*International Student Support*

Basic guidance and resources for students preparing to begin their academic journey in Korea, helping them adapt more smoothly to their new environment.
